Our logo represents the core of our brand identity. Use it consistently across all touchpoints to build brand recognition.

Symbol Only

Copied!

Clearspace

Always maintain adequate clearspace around the logo to ensure visibility and impact. The minimum clearspace is equal to the height of the "X" in the logo.

Logo Clearspace Diagram

Minimum Size

To maintain legibility, never reproduce the logo smaller than the minimum sizes specified below:

  • Primary Logo: 100px wide for digital, 35mm for print
  • Symbol Only: 30px wide for digital, 10mm for print

Do's and Don'ts

Do

  • Use the logo in its original proportions
  • Maintain adequate clearspace around the logo
  • Use the white version on dark or colored backgrounds
  • Use the symbol alone when appropriate

Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Add effects like shadows or glows
  • Place the logo on busy backgrounds
  • Rotate or flip the logo

Color Usage Guidelines

Primary Colors

Our primary colors should be used for:

  • Teal: Accents, buttons, links, highlights
  • Blue: Secondary accents, gradients, icons
  • Black: Text, backgrounds, UI elements
  • White: Backgrounds, text on dark colors

Secondary Colors

Our secondary grays should be used for:

  • Dark Gray: Secondary text, backgrounds
  • Medium Gray: Borders, dividers, inactive states
  • Light Gray: Subtle backgrounds, disabled states

Brand Hierarchy

  • Product Brands: Boxcore, BX Studio, Boxcar Lingo, Boxcar Twin, WaveSight

Co-branding Guidelines

When displaying multiple product brands together:

  • Maintain consistent spacing between logos
  • Align logos by their baseline
  • Use the same background color for all logos
  • Maintain the master brand (Project Boxcar) prominence
